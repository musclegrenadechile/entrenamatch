**CRITICAL - DO THIS FIRST OR VOICE NOTES WILL 403:**
The voice upload rules (chat-voice path) are now fixed and well documented in storage.rules.
You **MUST** deploy/publish the storage rules after any code change that touches voice or before testing real uploads:

Option A (Console - recommended):
1. Go to https://console.firebase.google.com/project/entrenamatch/storage/rules
2. Replace ALL content with the full text from your local `storage.rules` file.
3. Click Publish. Wait 30-60s.

Option B (CLI):
npx firebase deploy --only storage --project entrenamatch

Without this step you will keep getting "storage/unauthorized" on voice notes even if the app code is perfect.
